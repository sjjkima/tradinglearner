How did crypto even originate: The truth behind Crypto


Cryptocurrency originated in 2009 with the creation of Bitcoin by the anonymous figure Satoshi Nakamoto, aiming to offer an alternative to traditional finance after the 2008 financial crisis. By using blockchain technology, Bitcoin allowed peer-to-peer transactions without the need for banks, offering greater transparency and security. Its success sparked the rise of thousands of other cryptocurrencies, including Ethereum, which expanded crypto's use beyond just payments. Though it started as a rebellion against centralized systems, crypto has since evolved into a major player in global finance, with ongoing debates about its future.
