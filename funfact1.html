"The Animal Kingdom of Stock Trading: A Wild World of Bulls, Bears, and More!"


A fun fact about trading is that the stock market actually has its own "animal kingdom"! Traders often use animal terms to describe different market behaviors: a "bull market" means prices are rising and optimism is high, while a "bear market" means prices are falling and there's a sense of pessimism. There are even "wolves" for aggressive traders, "sheep" for those who follow the crowd, and "rabbits" for quick, short-term traders. This quirky jargon adds a bit of personality to the financial world!
