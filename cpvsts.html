"Spotting the Difference: Candlestick Patterns vs. Trading Strategies"


Candlestick patterns are visual representations of price movements in financial markets, displayed through individual candles that indicate open, high, low, and close prices for a given time period. These patterns help traders predict potential price reversals or continuations based on historical data. Trading strategies, on the other hand, are comprehensive plans that traders use to make decisions, incorporating various factors like risk management, market conditions, and indicators, including candlestick patterns. While candlestick patterns focus on specific price action cues, trading strategies encompass broader techniques to achieve consistent results.
