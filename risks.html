"The reality of the Stock Market!"


The stock market works as a platform where investors can buy and sell shares of publicly traded companies. When a company wants to raise money, it can issue shares through an initial public offering (IPO), allowing the public to purchase a portion of the company. Once these shares are available, they can be traded on stock exchanges like the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE) or Nasdaq.
